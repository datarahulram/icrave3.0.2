FAQ

FAQs

1. What types of furniture does Icrave Creations offer ?

We offer a wide range of furniture for living rooms, bedrooms, dining rooms, offices, and outdoor spaces. Our products include sofas, coffee tables, beds, wardrobes, dining tables, desks, and patio sets, among others.

2. Where can I see your furniture in person ?

Currently, our showroom is located in Gurgaon. You can visit us to experience the quality and design of our furniture firsthand. For directions and visiting hours, please visit our website or contact our customer service.

3. How can I place an order ?

You can place an order directly through our website at [www.icravecreations.com](http://www.icravecreations.com). Simply browse our collections, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. For assistance, our customer service team is available to help.

4. Do you offer customization options ?

Yes, we offer customization on select furniture items. You can choose from different materials, finishes, and sizes to suit your preferences. Please contact our customer service team for more details on customization options.

5. What are your delivery options and charges ?

We offer various delivery options, including standard and expedited shipping. Delivery charges depend on your location and the size of your order. During checkout, you will see the available options and corresponding fees.

6. What is your return and exchange policy ?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your order, you can return or exchange it within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in their original condition. For more details, please refer to our return policy on our website.

7. Do you provide assembly services ?

Yes, we offer assembly services for most of our furniture. You can opt for this service during checkout. Our professional team will ensure your furniture is assembled correctly and safely in your home or office.

8. How can I track my order ?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your shipment on our website or the courier’s website. For any issues with tracking, please contact our customer service team.

9. Are your products environmentally friendly ?

10. How can I contact customer service ?

Our customer service team is available to assist you with any inquiries. You can reach us by phone, email, or through the contact form on our website. Visit our 'Contact Us' page for more information.
